Brownies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 100 g dark chocolate
  • 4 tsb butter
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Set oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. In another bowl, melt together chocoalte and butter.
  4. Pour wet ingredients into mix, stir untill smooth.
  5. Bake for 15 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.
